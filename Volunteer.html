Volunteer Opportunities

The Wheeler PTA plans and conducts several activities and events to build the Wheeler Elementary school community. We need volunteers to make these things happen. Please consider volunteering.

  • Room Parent - work closely with your child's teacher to assist with classroom celebrations and other projects
  • Walk-a-thon - assist the Walk-a-thon chairperson by helping with activity stations on the day of the walk (late Sept.)
  • Luncheons - help greet lunch visitors, assist with parking, set-up and clean-up for Grand Guest Lunch, Superman Lunch and Wonder Woman lunch
  • Wheeler Workers - meet first Thursday of each month to assist teachers with clerical tasks, copies, stapling, cutting forms, etc.
  • Picture Day Volunteer - assist children, photographers and teachers in getting everyone in front of the camera in a timely way
  • Vision/Hearing Screening Volunteer - assist with getting students to and from vision/hearing testing
  • Membership - assist with membership drives
  • Yearbook - volunteers will assist in yearbook sales, taking pictures at events, etc. to create a wonderful book of memories for students
  • Field Day - assist with field day activities in late May
  • PTA Board and Committees - provide leadership in planning and developing resources for PTA activities
  • Other - If you have a talent/strength that you believe would benefit the school, please let us know.

    • All volunteers need to complete a volunteer background check prior to volunteering.

    Become A Volunteer Today!

Simply create a user account and then select the volunteer opportunities that are of interest to you. You'll receive requests for specific events and reminders when you sign-up to volunteer at an event.
